How did CalCSAT come to life?

CalCSAT comes from the need of always being on top of our CSAT and creating solid reports every month, and every quarter.

At CalCSAT we are Technical Trainers with a vast amount of experience in the industry, we get you.

Technical Trainers

As Technical Trainers, we are always on calls with customers and partners. Enablement never rests and in order to be on top of deliverying great performances, asking for feedback is what we need. But how to meticulously calculate our CSAT every time we get to the end of a month or of a quarter? Sure enough, we can pull up our results, note down the grades we get, divide their sum by the maximum grade we could have achieved and multiply that number times 100. Look at that, we just spent a couple of minutes reading this, let alone doing it. Guess what? With CalCSAT you can let our solution handle all of that and in a matter of seconds you will know what your CSAT% looks like! We have 3 options available for you: one interface based on a 5-star evaluation system where you can keep track of how many 5 stars you got; a similar interface but simplified where you just need to type in the total score you got; as well as an interface based on a 10-star evaluation system that works similarly to the 5-star simplified option.
